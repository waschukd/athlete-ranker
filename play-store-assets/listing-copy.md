## Full description

Sideline Star Evaluator is the companion app for coaches, scouts, and evaluators running tryouts and skills assessments on the Sideline Star platform.

Sign in with the account your association set up for you, pick your session, and start scoring in seconds. Every feature in the app is designed for the reality of working on a cold rink, a sunny field, or a loud gym — with a tablet or phone in one hand.

**What you can do**

• Score live with your choice of tap buttons, numpad, or drag grid — whatever fits your flow
• Dictate scores and notes hands-free with on-device voice recognition
• Review your calibration against other evaluators before each session
• Compare athletes side-by-side with jersey color + number (no names to bias scoring)
• Work offline — scores save locally and sync when signal returns
• Switch between White / Dark teams and view only athletes you still need to score

**Who it's for**

This app is for invited evaluators of youth sports associations and service providers that use Sideline Star. Accounts are created by administrators — the app doesn't have a public sign-up.

Not an evaluator yet but want to bring Sideline Star to your association? Learn more at sidelinestar.com.

**Data and privacy**

Your scores and notes are stored securely on Sideline Star's servers and shared only with authorized staff in your association. Voice audio is processed live by your device's built-in speech recognition and is never sent to or stored by Sideline Star. Full details: sidelinestar.com/privacy

## Data safety

Data collected:
- **Personal info**: Name (if provided by admin), Email address — *required*, *linked to user identity*, *not shared*
- **App activity**: In-app actions (scores, notes) — *required*, *linked to user identity*, *not shared*
- **App info and performance**: Crash logs, diagnostics — *optional*, *not linked to user*, *not shared*
- **Device or other IDs**: Device ID — *optional*, *not linked to user*, *not shared*
- **Audio**: Voice or sound recordings — *optional*, *not linked to user*, *not shared*, **processed ephemerally (not stored)**. Note: add the explanation "Voice audio is processed by the device's built-in speech recognition for real-time transcription. Audio is not stored on Sideline Star servers."

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
