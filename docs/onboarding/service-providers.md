# Getting Started — Service Providers

A **Service Provider (SP)** runs evaluations on behalf of multiple client associations:
you manage a shared evaluator pool, see every client's schedule in one place, and keep
sessions staffed.

---

## 1. Your dashboard at a glance
Tabs across the top:
- **Associations** — your client clubs.
- **Master Schedule** — every session across all your clients, in one calendar.
- **Evaluator Pool** — your evaluators (approve, rate, hours, suspend).
- **Leads** — people with scoped admin access to specific associations.
- **Reports** — staffing & evaluator-efficiency reports.
- **Messages** — two-way messaging with your evaluators.

---

## 2. Add a client association
1. **Associations → New Client.** Enter the org name and the main contact (name + email).
2. Sideline Star **emails the contact an invite** to finish setting up their account
   (they set their own password — no temp passwords).
3. You'll see right away whether the invite **sent** — and if email isn't configured,
   you get a **copyable invite link** to send manually. No silent failures.

> The new association then sets up its age categories and uploads athletes
> (RAMP/TeamSnap/TeamLinkt rosters import automatically).

---

## 3. The schedule is shared automatically
When an association or director uploads or edits a schedule, it appears on your
**Master Schedule** instantly — there's no separate import. You can also **add, edit,
or cancel sessions** for any client directly from your side; when you do, the
association admin, directors, and any signed-up evaluators are all notified that *you*
made the change.

---

## 4. Build & manage your evaluator pool
- **Invite evaluators** by email, or share a **join code**.
- Approve/deny applicants from **Evaluator Pool**.
- Per evaluator you can see a **scorecard** (agreement %, bias, consistency), **session
  history**, **flags** (late cancels, no-shows), **ratings**, and **hours** (approve for pay).
- **Suspend / reinstate** as needed. Two late cancellations auto-suspend an evaluator.

### Keeping sessions staffed
- The **Master Schedule** shows spots-open per session.
- **Blast** open sessions to your available pool (those not already signed up, no strikes).
- When a session is added or someone drops, the system **auto-offers** the open spot to
  eligible, available evaluators.
- **Reports → Daily/Weekly staffing** flag understaffed sessions before game day.

---

## 5. Leads (delegating)
Use **Leads** to give a person scoped admin access to one or more of your associations —
your "regional lead" who runs several clubs through their own dashboard without seeing
the others.

---

## 6. Messaging
**Messages** lets you reach evaluators (one, several, or the whole pool) — delivered
in-app and by email. Evaluators can message you back, and replies show in your inbox.

---

## Quick tips
- **Invite didn't arrive?** Use the copyable link the New Client dialog shows you.
- **Don't re-import schedules** from clients — you already see them live.
- **Strikes:** a late cancel (<24h) is Strike 1; a second auto-suspends — you're emailed
  both times and can reinstate from the evaluator's page.
