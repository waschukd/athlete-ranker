# Getting Started — Associations & Directors

Welcome to **Sideline Star**. This guide walks an association admin through setting up
an evaluation, and explains what a director does day-to-day.

---

## Part A — Association Admin

### 1. Activate your account
You'll get an email — **"Finish setting up your account"**. Click it, set a password,
and you're in. (If a Service Provider runs your evaluations, they created your account;
if you signed up independently, you're approved by Sideline Star first.)

### 2. Your dashboard
You land on your **Association Dashboard**. Each **age category** (e.g. *U13 AA*) is a
card showing a status pill:
- 🟡 **Needs setup** — not finished configuring
- 🟢 **Active** — ready / running

Click **+ Add Age Category** to create one (just give it a name like "U15 AA").

### 3. The setup wizard (5 quick steps, mostly pre-filled)
Creating a category drops you into the wizard. Sensible defaults are already in — you
can click straight through and only the roster is truly required.

1. **Sessions** — how many sessions and their weights (defaults: 4 sessions). Tip: use
   **Auto-balance to 100%** if weights don't add up.
2. **Scoring** — scale (1–10), increment, and skill categories (Skating, Puck Skills,
   Effort/Compete, Hockey IQ by default). Toggle position tagging if you score by position.
3. **Athletes** — **upload your registration export** (RAMP, TeamSnap, or TeamLinkt).
   The importer auto-detects columns, splits combined names, pulls birth year from the
   birthdate, and lets you preview before importing. *Tip: upload only the division for
   this category — if your file has several, tick the right one (it pre-selects the match).*
4. **Schedule** — upload dates/times/locations (optional, can add later).
5. **Review** — launch.

### 4. Assign a Director (optional but recommended)
On the category, use **Assign Director** so a coordinator can run that age group. A
director can do everything needed to run the eval, scoped to their category.

### 5. Get evaluators in
- Share your **evaluator join code** (on the dashboard), or **Invite** evaluators by email.
- They sign up for the sessions they can attend.
- (If a Service Provider manages you, evaluators come from their shared pool.)

### 6. During the evaluation
- **Rankings** update automatically as scores come in.
- **Schedule** — add/cancel/move sessions anytime; everyone tied to a session is
  notified instantly, and freed-up spots are auto-offered to available evaluators.
- **Flags / Analysis** — review athletes where evaluators disagreed.
- **Create Final Teams** — once scoring is done, build teams from the rankings.
- **Parents** — send a welcome email and the schedule; player reports are available after.

---

## Part B — Directors

### What you can do
A director runs the evaluation for the age category(ies) you're assigned. From your
**Director Dashboard** you can:
- See **live rankings**, the **schedule**, **athletes**, and **analysis/flags**.
- **Add/cancel/move sessions** (everyone is notified automatically).
- **Edit scores** when needed (a short reason is required — keeps an audit trail).
- **Assign athletes to groups** and **build final teams**.
- Import athletes and notify parents.

### Multiple age groups?
If you're assigned to more than one category, use the **category switcher** dropdown in
your header to jump between them. It remembers the last one you were on.

### What only the association admin can do
- Change category **settings** (scale, categories, position tagging).
- **Assign/remove other directors.**

---

## Quick tips
- **Roster import:** export *one division* from your registration system when you can —
  it's the cleanest. The importer handles RAMP / TeamSnap / TeamLinkt formats.
- **Schedule changes are safe:** cancel a session and signed-up evaluators + your
  Service Provider are notified immediately; nothing is silently lost.
- **Stuck?** A category stuck on 🟡 *Needs setup* just means the wizard wasn't finished —
  open it and click through Review.
