# Getting Started — Evaluators

You evaluate players rink-side. This guide gets you from sign-up to scoring, including
what happens when the arena wifi is bad.

---

## 1. Join
- You'll either get an **invite email** or an **organization join code**.
- Sign up (name, email, password, code). Your account is **pending** until the
  association or service provider approves you — you'll be emailed when you're in.

---

## 2. Your dashboard
When you log in you see, top to bottom:
- **Score now** — your today's / next session, one tap to start scoring.
- **My Sessions** — grouped so what matters floats up:
  - **Today** · **Needs scoring** (a past session you haven't scored) · **Upcoming** · **Completed**
  - A small **key** explains the badges.
- **Available** — open sessions you can sign up for.
- **Availability** — mark days you're unavailable (use the 1 day / 1 week presets).
- **Messages** — message your provider; see their messages to you.

### Sign up / cancel
- **Sign up** for a session in one tap from **Available**.
- **Cancel** from My Sessions — you can add a quick reason (helps your provider).
  ⚠️ Cancelling **within 24 hours** is a late cancel (Strike 1); a second one suspends
  you. Mark **Availability** in advance so you're not offered sessions you can't take.

---

## 3. Scoring a session
Open the session (Score now, or the **Score** button on the card).

- **Find a player fast:** type their **jersey number** in the *Find #* box — the list
  jumps straight to them. (Players show by jersey + team color.)
- **Enter scores:** tap the score buttons (or use Numpad / Grid view, or **voice** on
  supported devices). Tap a value again to clear it.
- **Notes:** add a note per player; these feed the player's report.
- **Save state:** a chip always shows **All saved** / **Saving…** / **Offline** so you
  always know where your scores stand.

---

## 4. Bad wifi? You're covered.
The scoring screen is built for dead-zone arenas:
- **Every tap saves to your device instantly** — you can score with no connection at all.
- When you get signal back, scores **sync automatically**. You can also tap **Resync now**.
- Switching to a backup tablet? Log in there and open the same session — your scores
  **load back from the server** automatically.

### If something goes really wrong (rare)
On the scoring screen there's a small **Backup ▾** menu:
- **Download CSV** — a readable copy of your scores.
- **Download backup file** — a file you can restore on another device.
- **Restore from file** — load that backup into the session on any device; it then syncs.

You'd only ever need this if a device died after scoring fully offline. Normally you
never touch it — your scores save and sync on their own.

---

## Quick tips
- **Step outside to sync:** if the rink has no signal, your scores are safe on the
  device; pop outside for a moment and they'll upload (or hit **Resync now**).
- **Don't see a session?** It may not be today — check the **Upcoming** group, or sign
  up under **Available**.
- **Can't make it?** Cancel early (not within 24h) and mark your **Availability** so you
  aren't offered that date again.
