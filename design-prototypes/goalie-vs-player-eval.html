What's the same: the scoring screen, the per-category 1–10 buttons (your category scale/increment), notes, jersey check-in, voice/keyboard entry, and the live rankings that result.
What's different: a goalie evaluator's roster contains only goalies and shows the 5 goalie categories; a skater evaluator's roster contains only skaters and shows the 4 skater skills. (A category toggle can optionally let skater evaluators score goalies too — off by default.)
● Player categories: Skating · Puck Skills · Effort/Compete · Hockey IQ ● Goalie categories (standard template, 4): Skating/Balance/Agility · Positioning/Angles/Net Coverage · Feet/Hands/Stick/Rebounds · Anticipation/Reading the Play
